La Casamançaise Menu

Appetizers

  • Shrimp or Fish Pastels

    Small fried pastries filled with seasoned fish or shrimp.

  • Codfish or Fish Accras

    Fried fish fritters with herbs and spices.

  • Marinated Shrimp Skewers

    Shrimp marinated in lemon, garlic, and chili, then grilled.

  • Smoked Mangrove Oysters

    Fresh oysters smoked over a wood fire, served with lemon and chili.

Main Courses

  • Casamance-style Thiéboudiène

    Rice with fish and vegetables, enhanced with local spices.

  • Kaldou

    Fish cooked in a lemon-based sauce with garlic, onions, and bay leaves.

  • Ebou Ganaar (Smoked Chicken Stew)

    Smoked chicken slow-cooked with local spices, vegetables, and palm oil.

  • Shrimp or Fish Caldou

    Shrimp or fish cooked in a tangy lemon sauce with vegetables and chili.

  • Smoked Oyster Rice

    Flavored rice served with smoked mangrove oysters.

  • Casamance-style Mafé

    A rich peanut butter sauce with fish or meat, served with rice.

Desserts

  • Bounoukan

    Fermented rice dough mixed with coconut milk and sugar, often flavored with vanilla or nutmeg.

  • Casamance-style Thiakry

    Sweet millet couscous mixed with curdled milk, sugar, grated coconut, and sometimes dried fruits.

  • Coconut Rice Pudding (Sombi)

    Rice cooked in coconut milk with sugar and cinnamon.

  • Millet Flour Cakes (Ngalakh)

    Made with millet, peanut paste, sugar, and curdled milk, often enjoyed during celebrations.

  • Cashew Apple Jam

    A jam made from cashew apples, cooked with sugar and lemon.

  • Corn Porridge with Coconut Milk (Fondé)

    Thick porridge made from fermented corn, coconut milk, and sugar.

  • Fresh Tropical Fruits

    Mangoes, papayas, guavas, and soursop, often served with a drizzle of lemon and honey.

  • Homemade Bissap or Baobab Ice Cream

    Refreshing sorbet made from hibiscus flowers or baobab fruit.
